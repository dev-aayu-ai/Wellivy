Weekly Full-Body Fitness Plan

Day Focus Area Activities Duration
Monday Strength + Mobility
  • Warm-up (5 mins)
  • Bodyweight squats – 3×15
  • Push-ups – 3×12
  • Plank – 3×30 sec
  • Hip mobility drills – 5 mins
  • Cool-down stretches – 5 mins
 30–40 mins
Tuesday Flexibility + Mental Health
  • Breathwork (box breathing – 5 mins)
  • Full-body yoga flow (Sun salutation ×5)
  • Neck, spine, hamstring stretches
  • Guided meditation – 10 mins
 30–45 mins
Wednesday Core + Balance + Flexibility
  • Warm-up (jumping jacks, ankle rolls – 5 mins)
  • Plank to push-up – 3×10
  • Leg raises – 3×15
  • Bird-dog – 3×12 per side
  • Standing balance pose (Tree pose)
  • Cool-down yoga stretches – 5 mins
 35–40 mins
Thursday Mindfulness + Recovery
  • Slow-paced yoga (yin yoga or long stretches)
  • Breathwork (alternate nostril – 5 mins)
  • Mindful walking (10–15 mins)
  • Gratitude journaling (optional)
 30–45 mins
Friday Full Body Strength + Endurance
  • Warm-up (5 mins)
  • Burpees – 3×10
  • Push-ups – 3×15
  • Lunges – 3×10/leg
  • Wall sit – 3×30 sec
  • High knees – 1 min
  • Cool-down – 5 mins
 40–45 mins
Saturday Flexibility + Strength Yoga
  • Dynamic yoga flow (Warrior series, Cobra, Cat-Cow)
  • Isometric holds (Chair pose, Boat pose – 30 sec each)
  • Breathwork – 5 mins
 40 mins
Sunday Rest + Light Activity
  • Nature walk / light jog – 20 mins
  • Stretching (optional)
  • Reflection or meditation – 10 mins
 20–30 mins
Designed for holistic development – strength, flexibility, calmness, and focus
